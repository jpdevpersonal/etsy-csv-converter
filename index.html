Turn Your Etsy CSV Into a Clean Monthly Report

Upload your Etsy payment CSV, and we turn it into a tidy month-by-month breakdown — ready to share with your accountant or add straight to your books.

No sign-up needed Works in your browser Download as Excel or CSV Up to 13 files at once
How it works

1. Go to Etsy → Shop Manager → Finances → Payment Account → Download CSV.

2. Drop that file into the tool below.

3. Download your clean monthly accounts spreadsheet.

100% private — your CSV is processed in your browser and is never sent anywhere.
